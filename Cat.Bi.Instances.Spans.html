Let C\ca{C}C be a precategory. By a span in C\ca{C}C (from an object A:CA : \ca{C}A:C to an object B:CB : \ca{C}B:C), we mean a diagram of the form A‚ÜêC‚ÜíBA \ot C \to BA‚ÜêC‚ÜíB.
